# MeloFi — Make. Mix. Master.

**MeloFi** is a digital music platform that lets you **listen, create, learn, and earn** — all in one place. It works on both **web and mobile**, so you can enjoy music wherever you are.  

With MeloFi, you can:  
- **Listen** to songs and playlists from around the world  
- **Remix or edit** songs legally and share your creations  
- **Create your own music** and collaborate with others  
- **Learn instruments and music skills** through interactive lessons  
- **Connect with other music lovers and artists**  
- **Earn rewards** through music streams, remixes, and courses  

MeloFi makes music **fun, creative, and fair** for everyone — whether you are a listener, a beginner creator, or a professional artist.
